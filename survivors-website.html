About Survivors

Survivors is an action-packed survival game where you face endless waves of monsters. As time progresses, your enemies become stronger and more numerous. Collect experience, level up, and choose new weapons and abilities to survive longer in this challenging world.

"How long can you survive in a world overrun by monsters? Adapt, upgrade, and overcome impossible odds in this addictive survival game."

How to Play

Controls

  • Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character
  • Weapons fire automatically at the nearest enemies
  • Collect gems to gain experience and level up
  • Press ESC or P to pause the game

Tips

  • Keep moving to avoid being surrounded
  • Focus on complementary upgrades for your build
  • Prioritize enemies that pose the greatest threat
  • Collect all experience gems before they disappear

Game Features

Multiple Characters

Choose from different characters with unique starting weapons and abilities.

Weapon Upgrades

Discover and upgrade various weapons and passive items to create powerful combinations.

Challenging Enemies

Face different types of monsters with unique behaviors and abilities.

Gameplay

Progression System

As you defeat enemies, you'll collect experience gems that allow you to level up. Each level grants you a choice of upgrades that can dramatically change your gameplay strategy.

Dynamic Difficulty

The longer you survive, the more challenging the game becomes. Enemies will grow stronger, appear in larger numbers, and new enemy types will be introduced.

Build Variety

With dozens of weapons and passive items to discover, no two runs are the same. Experiment with different builds to find the most powerful combinations.

Special Events

Watch out for special events and boss battles that will put your skills to the ultimate test. Defeat powerful bosses to earn unique rewards.
